Project Spotlight

Video2LoRA

Unified semantic-controlled video generation via per-reference-video LoRA.

A transformer HyperNetwork predicts a dedicated semantic LoRA from a reference video, enabling zero-shot control without per-condition fine-tuning.

Video2LoRA reframes semantic video control as per-reference-video adaptation: instead of training or storing separate heavy condition-specific modules, it predicts compact LoRA weights that plug into a frozen diffusion backbone.
